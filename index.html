My name is Matt Watson and I am from the United Kingdom.
I was only 16 when I decided to buy my first photographic camera.
I've been taking pictures ever since.

My life & hobbies are full and diverse - I am a divemaster with over 15 years of experience,
an avid rollerblader, lifelong adventurer & explorer, but one constant in my life
for over 20 years now has been my camera. Currently I carry a Nikon D7200 and use it
to shoot everything that catches my eye on my travels, whether it is beautiful scenery,
vibrant wildlife (of the human variety too) or anything inbetween.

This is my gallery - please feel free to look around and enjoy my work,
all of which is available for licensing or printing & delivery.

Hello, I am Matt Watson.  
Sailor. Photographer. Divemaster.  
Blader. Explorer. Adventurer.  

Welcome to my photo gallery. 
Have a look, enjoy my work  
& feel the wind in your sails.
